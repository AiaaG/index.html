                                    Dear mom,

                   I sincerely wish you a happy birthday.
                   And I just wanted you to know, that:             
            
      You are intelligent.
            
      You are strong.
            
      You are fabulous.
            
      You are kind.
            
      You are sweet.
            
      You are literally a walking encyclopedia.
            
      You are charming.

            
      You are beautiful.

            
      You are loved.

            
      And you will always be my beautiful, gentle, caring and loving mom.

            
      I might not always tell you this, but I love you, with all my heart,

            
      and you are the person who gave me life, love and support.

            
      Thank you. For everything you did.

            
      And I'm sincerely sorry for everything that made you sad.

            
      And trust me, your life will become better and better.

            
      We already achieved the permanent residency of Argentina.

            
      We are living a stable life.

            
      Don't worry too much.

            
      Please always remember that I'm on your side,'cause we're besties 4ever.

            
      You really need to smile more often, 

            
      because according to Sigal Barsade's research known as affective revolution,

            
      people affect each other's emotions. You can find more information here.
         
            
      Lastly, thank you for reading, and I hope you will have a splendid birthday.

            
                                                         Love,

            
                                                         Aiana.
